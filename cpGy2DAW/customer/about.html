About Us

We're a mobile app that lets you schedule a car wash or other service in advance. Our revolutionary approach is based on a user-friendly interface and tight integration with top-tier vehicle wash facilities.

Our ultimate goal is to make washing cars easier for everyone. Our app can help if you're in a hurry, don't want to leave work, or don't want to hunt for a vehicle wash.

Find a car wash near you, compare prices, and schedule an appointment with ease with our convenient app. The app's notifications of any changes to the status are instantaneous.

In an effort to better serve our customers, we are constantly looking for new avenues of growth. Our car wash appointment software is reliable and easy to use.
